About the Project

This website is part of a master's thesis in Data Science at the University of Namur, focusing on the digital transformation of SMEs.

The Objective

This website helps assess the digital maturity of SMEs through two main axes, divided into several dimensions and measurable KPIs.

How does it work?

To begin, a company account must be created by a manager. Once registered, the manager receives a unique Company Code.

This code can be shared with employees, allowing them to answer the survey without creating their own account.

The survey asks role-specific questions to both managers and employees.

How can I see my results?

Once all participants complete the survey, the company owner can log in, click on "My Results", and generate a full maturity report based on the latest data.
