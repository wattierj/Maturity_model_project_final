Why this questionnaire?

This project is part of a master’s thesis in Data Science at the University of Namur. It aims to evaluate the digital maturity of SMEs through a structured diagnostic.

How does it work?

  • 1️⃣ The manager creates an account and receives a unique company code.
  • 2️⃣ Employees use this code to answer the survey according to their role.
  • 3️⃣ Once completed, a comprehensive report is generated to visualize digital maturity.

What is the objective?

To help SMEs identify their strengths and weaknesses in digital transformation and provide personalized recommendations for improvement.
